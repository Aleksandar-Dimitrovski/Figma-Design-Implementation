How We Will Work Together?

  • 1
    Introductory Call

    We will kick things off with an introductory video chat so that I can learn about your problem and opportunity.

  • 2
    Project/Contract Start

    Once we are aligned with the scope of work and necessary paperwork, I will get to work on strategy and deliverables.

  • 3
    Scope Of Work

    I will research your business and target market, develop a profile of your target market to understand their behavior and preferences, and build a tailored scope of work aligned with your goals.

  • 4
    Consistent Communication

    Expect regular (weekly) communication from me. I will share milestones, ask for feedback, and we will discuss every design step together. You will approve each stage.

  • 5
    Wrap Up

    I will share the final deliverables with you. You will approve everything stage by stage.
